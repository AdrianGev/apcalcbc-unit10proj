How to Play

Goal

Defend your base from enemies by answering calculus questions correctly. Correct answers earn coins to buy towers. Wrong answers let enemies through!

Towers & Questions

Questions: Answer multiple choice questions to earn coins. Each topic unlocks new towers.

Towers: Click empty spaces on the map to place towers. Each tower type corresponds to a calculus topic (Geometric, p-Series, etc.).

Upgrades: Click existing towers to upgrade them using earned coins.

Enemies & Waves

Start Wave: Click "START WAVE" to begin enemy attacks.

Time Freeze: Buy time freezes (50 coins) to pause enemies and give yourself time to answer questions.

Speed: Toggle between 1x and 2x game speed.

Dashboard & Review

Dashboard: View your accuracy and coins per topic. Pin a topic to focus on it.

Full Review: Click "FULL REVIEW" to cycle through all questions without enemies attacking.
